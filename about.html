About Us

The misson of SongStars

The goal of SongStars is to give music lovers a community to share their opinions and learn other people's opinions on different songs. Through SongStars users can find and explore new music, share their views, keep track of their favorite songs, and engage with other music lovers!

Our Team

Hi!! We are Owen Chestnut and Kyra Fox, and we are the founders of SongStars. We both share a passion for music and love talking about music, but felt there wasn't a platform to do so. Thus, we decided to make one ourselves. We hope you enjoy it as much we do!
